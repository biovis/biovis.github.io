Registration

Registration for BioVis 2011 is handled through the IEEE VisWeek 2011 registration system. Please visit the IEEE VisWeek 2011 registration page for details. One day and two day registrations are available.

Register by 16 September 2011 to receive a substantial early registration discount. See below for additional IEEE and ISCB membership discount information.

Discounts

Members of the IEEE, members of the International Society for Computational Biology (ISCB), and members of the ACM will receive a discount of up to 20% on their VisWeek registration. The organizers encourage BioVis 2011 participants to join either one of these organizations before registering to receive the discount and additional benefits.
