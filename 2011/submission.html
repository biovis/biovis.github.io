Submission

Please see Call for Participation for details about submission types and criteria.

Please prepare your submissions according to the instructions for IEEE Visualization & Graphics Technical Committee (VGTC) conferences. LaTeX and Microsoft Word templates are provided. Please note that the instructions for InfoVis and Vis papers are not relevant for submissions to BioVis.

Guidelines for the preparation of camera-ready papers (after acceptance) are also located on the website of the IEEE Visualization & Graphics Technical Committee.

Paper and Abstract Upload

BioVis 2011 is using the PCS submission system for both paper and abstract submissions.

  • Now closed! To submit an abstract go to https://precisionconference.com/~vgtc, log in, and select "Submit to BioVis 2011 Abstracts". Remember that all materials have to be submitted by July 8th, 5 PM PDT. Authors will be notified about the decision of the Program Committee by August 15th.
  • Now closed! To submit a paper go to https://precisionconference.com/~vgtc, log in, and select "Submit to BioVis 2011 Papers". Remember that all materials have to be submitted by April 30th, 5 PM PDT. Authors will be notified about the decision of the Program Committee by June 30th.
