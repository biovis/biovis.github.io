BioVis@ISMB Poster Award Winners

Published Jul 9, 2016

Nils Gehlenborg

Chanin Tolson Woods is the winner of the BioVis@ISMB 2016 Best Poster Award sponsored by F1000 for her poster on “Visual exploration of the functional consequence of structure and structure change in RNA ensembles”.

Runner-ups were Andreas Dräger (“New Standard Resources for Systems Biology: BiGG Models Database and Visual Pathway Editing with Escher”) and Kuntal Kumar Bhusan (“Innovative methods for visualization of multidimensional ‘omics data”). See the list of poster abstracts for details.
