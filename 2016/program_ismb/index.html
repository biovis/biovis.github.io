Program BioVis@ISMB

BioVis@ISMB 2016 Program

July 8, 2016

The BioVis SIG Meeting at ISMB 2016 will be a one day event.

The program will feature a keynote by Janet Kelso from the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology, an invited capstone speaker, multiple sessions with oral presentations selected from submissions to the New Research and Highlight Tracks, as well as a poster session combined with a reception.

Keynote Speaker: Janet Kelso

Janet Kelso is a group leader in the Department of Evolutionary Genetics at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology in Leipzig, Germany. Additional keynote details will be posted shortly.
