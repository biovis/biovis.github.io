Program BioVis@ISMB

BioVis@ISMB 2016 Program

The BioVis SIG Meeting at ISMB 2016 will be a one day event.

The program will feature an invited keynote speaker, an invited capstone speaker, multiple sessions with oral presentations selected from submissions to the New Research and Highlight Tracks, as well as a poster session combined with a reception.

July 8, 2016

Program details will be announced closer to the event.
